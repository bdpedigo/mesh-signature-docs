Extensions and related work

  • Wave kernel signature (Aubry, Schlickewei, and Cremers 2011)
  • Prior work on RF on kernel signatures (Rodola et al. 2014)
  • Scale-invariant kernel signatures (M. M. Bronstein and Kokkinos 2010)
  • Higher-order aggregations on top of signatures
    • Bag of features/TF-IDF (A. M. Bronstein et al. 2011)
  • Discriminant analysis on eigenfunctions (Litman and Bronstein 2014)
  • Skeletonization (Au et al. 2008)
  • Semantic skeletonization (Meyer et al. 2023; De Goes, Goldenstein, and Velho 2008)
  • Chebyshev approximation or numerical integration methods (Bähr, Dachsel, and Breuß 2018; Köhler and Breuß 2021)
  • General spectral filters on shape (Vallet and Lévy 2008)
  • Mesh simplification (Lescoat et al. 2020)
  • Neural network extensions
    • DiffusionNet (Sharp et al. 2022)
    • HodgeNet (Smirnov and Solomon 2021)
